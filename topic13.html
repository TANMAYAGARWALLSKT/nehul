SCAMPER

We dived into the fascinating world of Scamper. It's all about boosting creativity and coming up with new ideas. Basically, it's like a set of strategies to help us think differently about things. We went through each letter in "Scamper" and talked about what it stands for:

- S: Substitute - this is all about swaping things out.

- C: Combine - this encourages thinking about how different elements can be merged or combined to create something new.

- A: Adapt - this focuses on how the subject can be adapted or modified to fit different circumstances or needs.

- M: Modify - this involves changing or altering certain aspects or properties of the subjects.

- P: Put to another use - Products can often be used for more than one purpose.

- E: Eliminate - eliminate unnecessary elements from our idea to make it simpler and more focused.

- R: Reverse/Rearrange - Look at ideas from different angles or rearrange its components to see what the new possibilities emerge.

For each letter, we discussed how we can apply it to whatever we are working on.
