About

Creative thinking skills are essential abilities that enable individuals to think in unique and imaginative ways. These skills involve generating new ideas, making connections between disparate concepts, and approaching problems from different angles. Creative thinking is not limited to artistic endeavors but is also crucial in fields such as science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), where innovative solutions are often required. Developing creative thinking skills can enhance problem-solving abilities, foster innovation, and promote a deeper understanding of complex issues.

Mission

The mission of a subject like Creative Thinking Skills is typically to enhance students' ability to think innovatively, critically, and imaginatively. It aims to develop skills such as problem-solving, idea generation, and lateral thinking, which are valuable in various personal, academic, and professional contexts. The subject may also focus on fostering a creative mindset, encouraging students to explore new perspectives, embrace ambiguity, and approach challenges with curiosity and open-mindedness.

Vision

The vision of developing creative thinking skills in a subject like design, fashion, or technology, as taught at NIFT, likely involves nurturing the ability to ideate, innovate, and problem-solve in novel and imaginative ways. It includes encouraging students to think beyond conventional boundaries, experiment with new ideas, and approach challenges with a fresh perspective. Creative thinking skills also involve fostering a mindset that embraces curiosity, flexibility, and the willingness to take risks, all of which are essential for success in creative fields.
