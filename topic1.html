Nine Dot

Certainly! Here's a classic puzzle that involves connecting 9 dots with 4 straight lines, without lifting your pen from the paper:

1. Start by drawing a line that connects the dots 1, 2, and 3 in a row. 

2. Next, draw a line that starts from dot 4 and goes to dot 6, but continue the line beyond dot 6, extending it to connect with dot 9.

3. Lastly, draw a line from dot 7 to dot 2, but continue this line beyond dot 2 to connect with dot 5.

This solution requires you to think "outside the box" by extending the lines beyond the perceived boundaries of the square formed by the dots. This way, you can connect all 9 dots with just 4 straight lines.
