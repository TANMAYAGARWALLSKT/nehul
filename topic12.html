RESOURCE CENTRE VISIT

We went to the resource centre and were given different tasks.

Task 1 - We were then given a book to each group and were asked to discuss on how to creatively sell that particular book in 2 minutes . We were also said to mark others on their selling techniques. The task was fun and mind juggling.

We as a group decided to mark each group on a specific criteria. We searched on key points of HOW TO SELL A BOOK EFFECTIVELY and found some key tips on which we judged each group .

The book given to us was THE POWER OR CREATIVE INTELLIGENCE.
